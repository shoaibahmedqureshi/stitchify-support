Frequently Asked Questions

How do I stitch images together?

Tap "Add Images" or "Pick Images" on the home screen, select your photos or screenshots, then tap the stitch icon (⇪) in the top bar to combine them into one long image.

Why is there a watermark on my exported image?

The watermark appears on free-tier exports. Upgrade to Stitchify Premium to remove it permanently.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings (gear icon) → tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple/Google account used for the original purchase.

Why is my image limited to 3 photos?

The free tier allows up to 3 images per stitch. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited images per stitch.

How do I cancel my subscription?

On iPhone: Settings app → your Apple ID → Subscriptions → Stitchify → Cancel. On Android: Play Store → Subscriptions → Stitchify → Cancel.
